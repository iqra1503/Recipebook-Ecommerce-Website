Mango Lassi

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 3

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. If using fresh mangoes, peel and chop them into small pieces. If using canned mango pulp, measure out 1 cup.
  2. n a blender, combine the chopped mangoes (or mango pulp), Greek yogurt, milk, sugar (or sweetener), and cardamom powder (if using).
  3. Add ice cubes if you want a chilled lassi. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Taste the lassi and adjust the sweetness if necessary by adding more sugar or sweetener.
  5. Pour the mango lassi into glasses. Garnish with mint leaves if desired.
  6. Enjoy your refreshing and creamy Mango Lassi!
