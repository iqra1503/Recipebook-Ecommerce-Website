Chicken Kebab

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 80 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, combine 1/2 cup yogurt, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, 4 minced garlic cloves, 1 tablespoon minced ginger, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon cumin powder, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, 1 teaspoon garam masala powder, 1 teaspoon paprika, salt to taste, 1/2 teaspoon black pepper, 2 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh coriander leaves, and 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint leaves (if using).
  2. Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix well to ensure all the pieces are coated evenly.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight for the best flavor.
  4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat or your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  5. Thread the marinated chicken cubes onto the soaked wooden skewers or metal skewers, leaving a little space between each piece.
  6. Grilling: Place the skewers on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on the edges.
  7. Baking: Place the skewers on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil or parchment paper. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, turning once halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on the edges. You can also broil for the last 2-3 minutes for extra charring.
  8. Serve the chicken kebabs hot with naan or pita bread, a side of salad, and your favorite dipping sauce such as mint chutney or yogurt sauce. Enjoy your delicious chicken kebabs!
