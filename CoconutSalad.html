Coconut Quinoa Salad

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa, coconut milk, water, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the quinoa is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  2. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  3. While the quinoa is cooking and cooling, prepare the vegetables: dice the red bell pepper and cucumber, finely chop the red onion, grate the carrot, and halve the cherry tomatoes.
  4. Chop the fresh cilantro and mint (if using) and dice the avocado.
  5. Toast the shredded coconut in a dry skillet over medium heat, stirring frequently, until golden brown and fragrant. Set aside to cool.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together the lime juice, olive oil, maple syrup, salt, and black pepper.
  7. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled quinoa with the diced red bell pepper, cucumber, finely chopped red onion, grated carrot, halved cherry tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and mint (if using).
  8. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine. Add the diced avocado and toasted shredded coconut and toss gently to mix.
  9. Serve the Coconut Quinoa Salad chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy it as a light main dish or a refreshing side dish.
