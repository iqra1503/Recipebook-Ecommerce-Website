Iced Coffee

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Brew a strong cup of coffee using your preferred method (drip, French press, espresso, etc.).
  2. Allow the coffee to cool to room temperature or refrigerate it until chilled.
  3. Fill a glass with ice cubes. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add the milk to the glass.
  5. Stir in sugar or sweetener to taste. If using vanilla extract, add it now and stir well.
  6. If desired, top your iced coffee with a dollop of whipped cream for an extra treat.
  7. Serve the iced coffee immediately with a straw or stirrer. Enjoy your refreshing iced coffee!
