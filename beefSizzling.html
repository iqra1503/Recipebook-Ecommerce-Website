Beef Sizzling

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 60 minutes

Total Servings: 3

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, 2 minced garlic cloves, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, preferably longer for better flavor.
  3. In a small bowl, combine 1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 tablespoons oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce, 4 minced garlic cloves, 1 tablespoon minced ginger, 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, and 1 tablespoon brown sugar. Mix well.If a thicker sauce is desired, dissolve 1 tablespoon of cornstarch in 2 tablespoons of water and set aside.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or sizzling plate over medium-high heat. Add the sliced bell peppers, onion rings, and mushrooms (if using). Stir-fry until they are tender but still crisp. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and 1 tablespoon of butter. Add the marinated beef and cook over high heat until the beef is browned and cooked through, about 3-5 minutes.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium. Add the prepared sizzling sauce to the beef in the skillet. Stir well to coat the beef. If using, add the cornstarch mixture to thicken the sauce. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the beef.
  7. Add the cooked vegetables back to the skillet and mix well with the beef and sauce.Transfer the beef and vegetable mixture to a preheated sizzling plate for serving.
  8. Garnish with chopped green onions. Serve immediately with lemon or calamansi wedges on the side for squeezing over the sizzling beef.
