Vegan Burger

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Total Servings: 3

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. In a small bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of ground flaxseed with 6 tablespoons of water. Mix well and let it sit for 5-10 minutes to thicken.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher, leaving some chunks for texture.
  3. Add the cooked brown rice, ground oats, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, grated carrot, corn kernels (if using), soy sauce, smoked paprika, cumin powder, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Add the prepared flax egg to the mixture and mix everything thoroughly until well combined.
  5. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty, about 1/2 inch thick. If the mixture is too sticky, you can wet your hands slightly to shape the patties.
  6. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Place the patties in the skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and heated through.
  7. In a small bowl, combine vegan mayo, ketchup, Dijon mustard, and garlic powder. Mix well.
  8. Toast the burger buns lightly if desired. Spread a generous amount of the prepared sauce on the bottom half of each bun.
  9. Place a lettuce leaf, a cooked patty, tomato slices, onion rings, pickles, and avocado slices (if using) on the bottom half of the bun. Top with the other half of the bun.
