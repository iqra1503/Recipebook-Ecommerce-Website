Ice Cream Sundae

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Scoop 3 generous scoops of your favorite ice cream flavors into a bowl or sundae dish.
  2. Drizzle the chocolate sauce and caramel sauce over the ice cream.
  3. Top with a generous amount of whipped cream. Sprinkle the chopped nuts, sprinkles, and crushed cookies or wafers over the whipped cream.
  4. Add fresh fruit if desired. Sprinkle mini marshmallows if using.
  5. Finish off with 2-3 maraschino cherries on top
  6. Serve immediately with a spoon. Enjoy your delightful and indulgent ice cream sundae!
