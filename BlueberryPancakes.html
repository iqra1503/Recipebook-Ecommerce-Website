Blueberry Pancakes

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 2

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, 1/4 teaspoon of salt, and 1 tablespoon of sugar until well combined.
  2. In another bowl, beat 1 large egg.
  3. Add 3/4 cup of milk and 2 tablespoons of melted butter to the beaten egg. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients.Stir until just combined. The batter should be slightly lumpy, so be careful not to overmix.
  5. Gently fold 1/2 cup of fresh or frozen blueberries into the batter.
  6. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat.Lightly grease it with a small amount of butter or oil.
  7. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake onto the hot griddle or skillet.Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes.Flip the pancakes and cook until the other side is golden brown, about 1-2 minutes more.
  8. Transfer cooked pancakes to a plate and keep warm while you cook the remaining batter.Serve the blueberry pancakes warm with maple syrup, additional butter, or your favorite toppings.
