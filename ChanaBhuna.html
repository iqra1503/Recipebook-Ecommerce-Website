Chola Bhuna

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. If using canned chickpeas, rinse them thoroughly under cold water and drain. If using dried chickpeas, soak them overnight, then cook until tender.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté for 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  6. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and oil starts to separate from the masala.
  7. Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan. Mix well with the masala, ensuring the chickpeas are coated evenly. Cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add garam masala powder and stir to combine. Cook for another minute to let the flavors meld together.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve Chana Bhuna hot with rice, roti, or any bread of your choice.
