Strawberry Smoothie

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Total Servings: 5

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. If using fresh strawberries and banana, you can add ice cubes for a thicker, colder smoothie.
  2. If using frozen strawberries and banana, you can skip the ice cubes.
  3. In a blender, combine the strawberries, banana, Greek yogurt, milk, honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract (if using).
  4. Add ice cubes if desired. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy.
  5. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add a bit more milk and blend again until you reach the desired consistency.
  6. Pour the strawberry smoothie into glasses. Sprinkle chia seeds on top (if using) for added texture and nutrition.
