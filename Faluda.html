Faluda

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Total Servings: 3

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Soak the basil seeds in water for about 30 minutes until they swell and become gelatinous. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the falooda sev according to the package instructions. Typically, it involves boiling the sev for a few minutes until soft. Drain and rinse under cold water. Set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the milk and sugar over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. Let the milk cool completely. You can chill it in the refrigerator for faster cooling.
  4. In serving glasses, start by adding a tablespoon of rose syrup at the bottom of each glass. Add a spoonful of soaked basil seeds on top of the rose syrup.
  5. Next, add a layer of cooked falooda sev. Pour the sweetened and chilled milk into the glasses, filling them up to about three-quarters.
  6. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top of the milk. Garnish with chopped nuts, jelly cubes, and fresh fruits if using. Sprinkle a pinch of cardamom powder if desired.
  7. Serve the falooda immediately with a long spoon or a straw. Enjoy your delightful and refreshing Falooda!
