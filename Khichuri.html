Khichuri

Savor the flavors of tradition and innovation, one delicious recipe at a time

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Total Servings: 4

Ingredients

Recipe

  1. Rinse the rice and dal separately under running water until the water runs clear.Soak them in water for about 15-20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker or large pot over medium heat.Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and any optional whole spices (cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods, black peppercorns). Sauté until fragrant.
  3. If using, add asafoetida (hing) and sauté for a few seconds.Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add chopped ginger and green chili, and sauté for another minute.Add chopped tomatoes (if using) and cook until they become soft and mushy.
  5. Add the soaked and drained rice and dal to the pot.Add turmeric powder and salt to taste.Add mixed vegetables if using.Pour in the water and stir well.
  6. If using a pressure cooker, close the lid and cook for 2-3 whistles on medium heat. If using a pot, cover and cook on low to medium heat until the rice and dal are fully cooked and the khichuri reaches your desired consistency (add more water if needed).
  7. Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally if using a pressure cooker.
  8. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro (coriander leaves).Serve hot with a dollop of ghee on top, along with accompaniments like yogurt, pickles, or papad.
